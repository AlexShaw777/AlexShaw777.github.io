IT Dashboard for the NSROC Contract

This website is the center of all IT issues/requests for the entirety of the NSROC F10 building that houses scientists, machinists and business personnel. It consists of IT issues, Software Installations, Equipment and GSE Laptop tabs. Each are forms that are users based. One tab that is our biggest functionality is the Admin tab, being only seen by, you guessed it, Admins. Within the admin tab, there is everything to metrics, to equipment tracking and counting, to returned items. This site is protected by NASA's main site called the WOCR which is the Sharepoint hub where all areas of F10 collide and strucutre data to be used.
